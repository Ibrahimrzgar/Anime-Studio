Attack on Titan (Japanese: Shingeki no Kyojin)


Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama . It is set in a world where humanity lives in cities surrounded by enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids referred to as Titans. The story follows the adventures of Eren Yeager, his adopted sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their friend Armin Arlert, The series has been adapted into an anime television series produced by Wit Studio and Production I.G, which began airing in Japan on April 7, 2013. The anime has been met with critical acclaim, with praise for its intense action sequences, dark atmosphere, musical score, and emotional weight. It has also become a commercial success both in Japan and internationally, with over 60 million volumes of the manga sold as of 2020. A live-action film adaptation was released in 2015. A second season of the anime aired from April 2017 to June 2017; a third season aired from July 2018 to July 2019; and a fourth season aired from December 2020 to March 2021.
