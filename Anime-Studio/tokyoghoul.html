Tokyo Ghoul (Japanese: Tōkyō Gūru)


Tokyo Ghoul is a Japanese dark fantasy anime series based on the manga of the same name by Sui Ishida . It follows the story of Ken Kaneki, a college student who is attacked by a ghoul and left for dead. After being saved by a mysterious doctor, he discovers that he has become part ghoul himself and must now learn to survive in a world where humans and ghouls exist side by side. The series follows Ken as he struggles to find his place in this new world, while also trying to protect those he cares about from the dangers of both human and ghoul society. With its dark themes, intense action sequences, and complex characters, Tokyo Ghoul is an anime that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Tokyo Ghoul has been praised for its dark atmosphere and complex narrative. It has become one of the most popular anime series in recent years and has spawned several spin-off manga series as well as two live-action films.
